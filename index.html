Core Features

Everything a pastor needs
to understand their congregation

From the first "Amen" to the final altar call — SermonIQ captures, analyzes, and transforms every moment of your service into actionable ministry intelligence.

🎙️
Live Transcription
Real-time speech-to-text captures every word of your sermon with speaker diarization, timestamp precision, and multilingual support. Full transcripts stored permanently in the cloud.
🔥
Response Detection
Detects Amen, Alleluia, "Preach!", applause, laughter, and weeping. Each response is timestamped and scored against the sermon transcript to reveal your most impactful moments.
📖
Scripture Intelligence
Semantic AI matches sermon themes to relevant Bible verses in real time. Supports KJV, NIV, ESV, NKJV, and NLT. The right verse surfaces at exactly the right moment.
📊
Engagement Analytics
Visual engagement heatmaps, keyword frequency charts, and congregation sentiment scores give pastors a complete picture of every service within 30 minutes of closing prayer.
🧠
Sermon Reframing Coach
AI-powered coaching identifies what resonated most and suggests structural improvements for next week — story placement, opening hooks, transition timing, and language style.
🗓️
Annual Service Planner
Aggregate a full year of service data into a 52-week sermon calendar, congregation spiritual profile, and seasonal trend report that shapes your entire ministry vision.
